About Puran Lifesciences

Founded in 1988, Puran Lifesciences has grown to become a global leader in the petroleum and life sciences industries, with operations spanning across five continents. Our mission is to deliver reliable energy solutions while pioneering sustainable practices and innovative life science applications that improve global health.
